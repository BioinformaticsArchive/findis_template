About the Country Node Website project


This site provides up-to-date information on XX monogenic diseases belonging to the NNNNN national/ethnic disease heritage. For each disease, the prevalence or incidence and a short description of clinical symptoms are provided, as well as genetic locus and a molecular description for identified mutations. The character and consequences of all known mutations, native and foreign, are described at the DNA and polypeptide level. Disease allele frequencies are typically reported for native mutations.

The target audience of the database is researchers and clinicians working in this field. Information for the database has been gathered from researchers, original and review publications, and reliable websites. The database follows the Quality Criteria for Health Related Websites recommended by the European Commission.

Funding for this database template has been provided by the Academy of Finland, Center of Excellence in Disease Genetics and the European Community's Seventh Framework Programme (FP7/2007-2013) under grant agreement number 200754 - the GEN2PHEN project.



This site uses live data from the Leiden Open Variation Database (LOVD) project. In many functional respects, this site is a filter or skin for LOVD. The site template design team worked closely with the LOVD team to provide these resources.
